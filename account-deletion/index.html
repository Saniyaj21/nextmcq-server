Account Deletion Request - NextMCQ

This page provides information about how to request deletion of your NextMCQ account. NextMCQ is a mobile application created by Saniyaj Mallik (hereby referred to as "Service Provider").

Important: Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. Only your user account will be deleted. Associated data like test attempts, questions, and tests may be retained (with identifying information removed or anonymized). Please read this page carefully before submitting a deletion request.

How to Request Account Deletion

To request deletion of your NextMCQ account, please follow these steps:

Step 1: Send an email request to our support team at saniyajmallik12@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request".
Step 2: Include the following information in your email:
  • Your registered email address associated with the NextMCQ account
  • Your full name (as registered in the app)
  • A clear statement that you want to delete your account
  • Any additional verification information if requested
Step 3: Wait for confirmation. Our team will process your request within 30 days and send you a confirmation email once your account has been deleted.

What Will Be Deleted

Upon account deletion, your user account will be permanently deleted. This includes:

  • Account Information: Your user account, name, email address, profile image, and authentication credentials
  • Profile Data: Your profile information, role, institute, subjects, referral code, and profile settings
  • Progress Data: Your coins, XP, level, and badges
  • Account Access: You will no longer be able to access your account or any account-related features

What Data May Be Retained

Please note that certain data associated with your account may be retained even after account deletion:

  • Test Attempts: Test attempts and results may be retained for historical and statistical purposes
  • Questions Created: If you are a teacher, questions you created may remain in the system (associated data will be anonymized)
  • Tests Created: If you are a teacher, tests you created may remain in the system (associated data will be anonymized)
  • Anonymized Analytics Data: Aggregated, anonymized usage data that cannot be linked to your identity may be retained for analytics purposes
  • Legal Compliance: Data required to be retained by law, regulatory requirements, or to resolve disputes may be kept as required
  • Backup Systems: Deleted account data may remain in our backup systems for up to 90 days before being permanently removed

Data Retention Period

After you submit an account deletion request:

  • Your user account will be marked for deletion immediately upon verification
  • Active deletion of your account from our primary systems will occur within 30 days of request verification
  • Account data in backup systems will be permanently removed within 90 days of the deletion request
  • After this period, your account cannot be recovered
  • Note: Associated data (test attempts, questions, tests) that may be retained will have identifying information removed or anonymized

Partial Data Deletion

If you wish to delete specific data without deleting your entire account, please contact us at saniyajmallik12@gmail.com with details about what data you would like removed. We will work with you to accommodate your request where technically feasible.

Response Time: We aim to respond to all deletion requests within 7 business days and complete deletion within 30 days.
